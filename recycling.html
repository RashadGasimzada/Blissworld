Did you know that only 31% of plastic waste is successfully recycled? Even more alarming -- by 2050, it's estimated that there will be more plastic in our oceans than animals.

We are taking action to help reduce that.

First Things First

If you see a 1, 2 or PET on the bottom of the bottle or jar, drop it in your local
recycling bin. All of our cartons and glass bottles are locally recyclable, too!

To take care of everything else (like pumps, jars, caps & droppers), we have
proudly partnered with TerraCycle®

This free, easy and innovative recycling program recycles all Bliss
packaging into new, useful items -- resulting in zero waste.

How It Works:

Register for our free recycling program at terracycle.com

Rinse out your empties, box them up and ship to TerraCycle® using their pre-paid label.

That's it! They take it from there and find innovative ways to recycle all of it.

Thank you for joining us in our continued commitment towards sustainability!
