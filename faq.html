Ordering & Payment

Payment Methods

We currently accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover Card and Bliss Gift Cards for Blissworld.com. orders.

When does my credit card get charged for my order?

Blissworld pre-authorizes your credit card at the time the order is placed. Once your order ships, your credit card is charged.

Canceling & Modifying Orders

Because your order is processed as soon as possible, we are unable to cancel or make changes to your order once it has been received. If you have any questions or concerns about your order, please contact Customer Service Toll + Stress Free Number at 888-243-8825, Mon-Fri 10am-12pm and 2pm-4pm PST or email us at customercare@blissmail.com.

International Orders

Blissworld.com does not accept orders outside of the US and Canada. If placing an order for Canada, a US form of payment must be used. All orders shipping to Canada will not include taxes & fees. These additional fees will be collected when your order arrives and is the customer’s responsibility. An order placed on Blissworld.com from another country will be automatically cancelled. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact customer service at 888-243-8825, Mon-Fri 10am-12pm and 2pm-4pm PST or email us at customercare@blissmail.com. Please note: our customer service team can only call out to US and Canada phone numbers.

Sales Tax:

  • Application of sales tax may be subject to change.
